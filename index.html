ABOUT HER

Jessica Walsh is a 37-year-old American Designer, Art Director, Illustrator, and Educator. She owns her own creative business agency “&Walsh” that she opened in 2019. She is in the .1% of women to own a design company.

  • ~ Walsh was born in 1986 in New York and was raised in Ridgefield, Connecticut.
  • ~ She found her passion at 11 years old she began coding & designing her own websites.
  • ~ As a young adult Walsh attended The Rhode Island School of Design where she graduated with her BFA degree.
